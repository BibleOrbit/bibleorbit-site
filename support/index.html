Bible Orbit Support

Bible Orbit support is available for app help, account questions, privacy questions, and account or data deletion requests.

Contact: support@bibleorbit.org

Please include the email address used for your Bible Orbit profile and a brief description of the issue or request.

What We Can Help With

  • Sign-in or profile access issues.
  • Reading progress, completion seals, wisdom points, or group sync questions.
  • Orbit group invitation and participation questions.
  • Privacy questions related to your Bible Orbit profile.
  • Account deletion or associated data deletion requests.

Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your Bible Orbit account or associated profile data, email support@bibleorbit.org from the email address connected to your profile when possible. If you are writing from another address, include enough information for us to identify the profile you want reviewed.

Privacy

Bible Orbit uses profile and reading progress data to support Scripture reading, progress tracking, and Orbit group features. Review the Privacy Policy for more detail about what is collected and how it is used.
